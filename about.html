About Natureland–YogChetna

Natureland–YogChetna Is A Serene Retreat In Galudih, Jamshedpur, Created To Help You Experience Authentic Yogic Living With Modern Facilities.

Rooted In The Five Points Of Yoga, Our Programmes Blend Daily Yoga, Meditation, Vegetarian Meals, And Spiritual Talks With The Simplicity Of Ashram Life.

Through This Collaboration Of Natureland And YogChetna, We Offer A Space To Recharge Your Body, Calm Your Mind, And Rediscover Inner Peace—Whether You Are A Beginner Or An Experienced Practitioner.

A Home for Balance, Healing, and Self-Discovery

Balance

Find Harmony Between Mind, Body, And Spirit Through Structured Daily Practices And Mindful Living.

Healing

Experience Natural Healing Through Yoga, Meditation, Ayurvedic Therapies, And Wholesome Nutrition.

Self-Discovery

Embark On A Journey Of Inner Exploration Through Spiritual Teachings And Contemplative Practices.

Our Philosophy

We Follow The Five Points Of Yoga Which Form The Foundation Of Our Daily Practice And Lifestyle:

  • Proper Exercise (Asanas)
  • Proper Breathing (Pranayama)
  • Proper Relaxation
  • Proper Diet (Vegetarian Nutrition)
  • Positive Thinking & Meditation

Our Daily Schedule Is Designed To Bring Balance, Discipline, And Inner Harmony To All Aspects Of Life.
