Embraced by the serenity of sacred forests, just a short distance from the worldly path of the National Highway and 20 km from Jamshedpur city, YogChetna – Natureland Wellness & Retreat stands as a haven of peace and transformation.

Guided by our Expert Yoga Gurus and Practitioners, we follow the divine Yogic Pathway of Vedamurty Taponistha Yogi Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya, bringing together the ancient spiritual essence of yoga with the harmony of nature and modern comfort.

Here, each day begins with the sacred Gayatri Mahayagya (Fire Ceremony) — a powerful ritual that awakens spiritual energy and inner harmony. Guided Yoga and Meditation sessions in the lap of nature bring deep peace to the mind and vitality to the body.

Our Satvik diet nourishes and energizes, while our rejuvenating Naturopathic therapies — including new, Steam Bath, Sauna, Shirodhara, Acupressure, and more — detoxify and revitalize both body and soul.

Guests can choose from luxurious modern Premium Cottage or cozy Garden Cottages, each designed to blend comfort with nature's tranquillity. Our Weekend and Weekly Wellness Programs offer a holistic experience of Yoga, Meditation, Yogic Satkarma, Naturopathy Detox, Satvik Dining, Satsang, Gayatri Yagya, Jungle Trails, and many soulful activities that guide you toward wellness and self-awareness.

At YogChetna – Natureland Wellness & Retreat, you are invited to rediscover your natural state of peace, health, and happiness — whether you come alone, with friends, or with family — to reconnect with your true self through the ancient path of yoga and the healing embrace of nature.

Program Guidelines & Discipline

At YogChetna-Natureland Yoga Retreat, every program is designed as a sacred journey towards harmony of body, mind, and soul. To receive the true essence and benefits of these practices, each participant is requested to follow the discipline with sincerity and devotion.

Induction Program

Upon arrival, all participants will attend an Induction Session, where detailed information about the retreat schedule, rules, and daily activities will be shared. This orientation will help you understand the flow of the program and prepare you for a balanced and mindful experience.

Discipline & Conduct

Discipline is the foundation of spiritual progress. During your stay, you are requested to maintain focus, silence during certain practices, and respect for the retreat environment. By following the set routine and remaining sincere in your participation, you allow the yogic energy to work deeply within you.

Fixed Schedule

Your rising and sleeping time will be fixed according to the retreat rhythm. Kindly adhere to these timings and attend all sessions on time — this consistency nurtures both inner balance and outer discipline.

Sattvik Diet

Throughout your stay, you will be served freshly prepared Sattvik meals, aligned with your daily schedule. Please consume only the meals provided, as they are designed to purify the body and support yogic and detox practices.

Participation & Classes

Attend all Yoga, Meditation, Naturopathic Detox, Sound Healing, and other scheduled sessions with full presence. Each activity complements the other, creating a complete circle of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Our Intention

Every rule and practice is crafted for your betterment. We invite you to participate wholeheartedly, with 100% sincerity and openness. When discipline meets devotion, true transformation begins.

Thank you — We welcome you with love and light — may your journey here bring peace, healing, and spiritual awakening.
