### Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ "Natureland YogChetna" (missing dash)
❌ "Natureland-YogChetna" (wrong dash type)
✅ "Natureland–YogChetna" (correct en-dash)

❌ "Services" (use "Yogic Activities")
❌ "Facility and Therapies" (singular)
✅ "Facilities & Therapies" (plural with &)

❌ "Naturapathic" (spelling error)
✅ "Naturopathic"

❌ "Satvic" (single 't')
✅ "Sattvic" (double 't')

❌ Using emojis in headings/buttons
✅ Using Font Awesome icons
